Norgad: When we needed a subregion to connect CC and DS, I knew exactly the theme I wanted to go for.<LINE>I was inspired by going on walks as a kid and seeing large puddles of stagnant water by the trails.<LINE>The water had this intense orange color to it, but was so incredibly still.

Norgad: I wanted a subregion that was basically a giant collection system for all the gunk that must build up in CC<LINE>The water goes through sump after sump and filter after filter as it's gradually clarified on its way down.

Norgad: I never ended up making any actual levels for the area, but I did make a schematic, with the general flow and shapes of rooms.
